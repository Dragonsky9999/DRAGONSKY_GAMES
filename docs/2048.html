What is 2048?

2048 is a simple but addictive puzzle game played on a 4×4 grid. The goal is to combine numbered tiles by sliding them in different directions—up, down, left, or right—until you create a tile with the number 2048!



How to play?

• Use your arrow keys (or swipe if you’re on a mobile device) to move all tiles in one direction.
• When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one with their combined value.
• Example: 2 + 2 → 4, 4 + 4 → 8, etc.
• After each move, a new tile (2 or 4) appears at a random empty spot.
• The game ends when there are no more valid moves—meaning the board is full and no adjacent tiles can be combined.



Tips

• Plan ahead! Just matching tiles quickly won’t get you far.
• Try to keep your largest tile in a corner.
• Avoid unnecessary moves that break your tile structure
